===
quasiquote with unquote and splice
===

`(a ~b ~@c)

---

(source
  (quasiquote
    (list

      (unquote_splicing
        (symbol)))))

===

---

(source
  (quasiquote
    (list
      (unquote_splicing
        (list
          (symbol)
          (number)
          (number)))
      (number))))
